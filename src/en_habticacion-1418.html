Habitación 1418

Every Saturday from 17 to 21h

Artists

MACBA 31 Collection

MACBA and the CCCB propose a new activity space for visitors aged between 14 and 18 years. Habitació 1418 (Room 1418) is an active meeting point that will open every Saturday from 5 to 10 pm. Conceived as a highly flexible platform, it will be the users themselves who will modulate its profile. The will be a continuous programme of activities and the free use of resources suggested by MACBA and the CCCB will be made available. The programme aims to reach young people in the metropolitan area and develop a special bond with El Raval.
