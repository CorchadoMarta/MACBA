Macba Collection

There's material that change with time, atmospheric conditions, the space they take, etc. Sometimes you can barely notice these changes and some other the opposite. In MACBA 31 collection we find pieces in which the artist doesn't take into account the environment, but represents it directly representing with it all kind of physical challenges.

From natural materials, some of the pieces and our own bodies, we will create actions that will lets us experimentate and observe the transformation of things and ourselves.
