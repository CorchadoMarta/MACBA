Cuerpos que hacen cosas

22 Jan to 05 Mar 2017

Artists

Marta Galán


Related events

MACBA 31 Collection

Do you want to dance with Trisha Brown while you hear a story from Vito Acconci with some earphones? Would you dare to dance along Wind and Songdelay from artist Joan Jonas with some simple every day elements such as wood, wheels, ropes and fans? Would you like to measure yourself like Ester Ferrer in her performance Intimate and personal? And why not dance to our past and future with 100 Jahre from the photgrapher Hans Peter Feldmann?

In Cuerpos que hacen cosas we will explore the MACBA Collection that research the peculiarities of language, actions and performance to use our body as the main instrument for art. We'll do it in a new way, through a program of 6 different sessions that will generate the necessary tools, so that all families involved have fun.
